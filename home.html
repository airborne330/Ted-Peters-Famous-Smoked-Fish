Many people are interested in the actual smoking process.

The raw fish are laid on racks over a smoldering fire of red oak.

Red oak, a native of Florida, is a very similar to the northern hickory.

The fish cook and smoke from 4 to 6 hours, depending on size and thickness of the fish.

A few years ago, only smoked mullet and Spanish mackerel was known.
Now many other kinds of fish are smoked and enjoyed, as well as the mullet and the mackerel.
